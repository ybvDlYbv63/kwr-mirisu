✧ Procedure ✧

  • Payment is either 100% upfront, or at least 50% upfront, 50% after the sketch.
  • For paypal, I must send an invoice that indicates it is a digital item.
  • I can finish around 1 - 40 days depending on my slots, education, and health. You will be notified in case of delays.
  • No refunds. However, you may ask for a refund in the instance I reached beyond the deadline with no updates.
  • I will send updates of the sketches for revisions. Please make sure to be as concise as possible.
  • If the client has stopped responding within 2 weeks upon update, it will be considered completed and there will be no refunds.
  • Back out period - 2 weeks maximum for a commission to stay on hold. When the invoice is not yet paid in two weeks, the slot is removed.
